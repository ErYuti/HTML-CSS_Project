sports_cricket Early Life

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 in Delhi into a Punjabi Hindu family. His father, Prem Kohli, worked as a criminal lawyer and his mother, Saroj Kohli, served as a housewife. He has an older brother, Vikas, and an older sister, Bhawna.Kohli's formative years were spent in Uttam Nagar. He commenced his early education at Vishal Bharti Public School. According to his family, Kohli exhibited an early affinity for cricket as a mere three-year-old. He would pick up a cricket bat, display natural skill, and request his father to bowl to him.

sports_cricket Cricket Journey

Virat Kohli's journey into the world of cricket began at a very young age. His passion and determination were evident from the start. He rapidly rose through the ranks in domestic cricket and announced his arrival on the international stage in August 2008 in a One Day International (ODI) against Sri Lanka.

sports_cricket Batting Maestro

Known for his aggressive and stylish batting, Virat Kohli has set numerous records and achieved milestones that few can match. Whether it's ODIs, Tests, or T20s, Virat consistently delivers exceptional performances, making him one of the most prolific run-scorers in the history of cricket.

sports_cricket Captaincy and Leadership

Virat's leadership qualities are equally impressive. He took over the captaincy of the Indian Test team in 2014 and later became the captain across all formats. His aggressive captaincy style has brought a new level of intensity and competitiveness to the Indian team.

sports_cricket Consistency and Records

What sets Virat Kohli apart is his incredible consistency. He has a remarkable ability to adapt to different formats and situations. His numerous records, including being the fastest to reach milestones, showcase not only his skill but also his mental toughness.

sports_cricket Off the Field

Beyond cricket, Virat is also known for his philanthropic efforts. He has been involved in various charitable activities and initiatives, using his influence to make a positive impact on society. His personal life, including his marriage to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, is often in the public eye, but he manages to maintain a balance between his professional and personal life.
